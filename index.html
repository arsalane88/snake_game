Snake

How to play

Welcome!

Press the key direction buttons on your keyboard to change the snakes direction of movement.

When the snake eats the food, it will grow 1 body part segment and you will receive 1 point.

Colliding with the walls or the snakes body will result in the game to be over.

Each 7 points gained will allow you to move up 1 level, were the snake will move faster.

The maximum level is 7.

Have fun!
